01 — About

Who I am

I'm Francesco, a 21 year old developer from Italy, currently studying Computer Science at Federico II.

I started coding out of curiosity and never stopped. I experiment a lot with AI tools. They help me learn faster, fill the gaps, and ship without getting stuck. That's how I move.

I call myself an indie hacker because that's the mindset: ship fast, learn from real usage, iterate. No waiting to be "ready enough".

Currently building, studying, and figuring it out one commit at a time. If you've got a project or an idea, let's talk.
